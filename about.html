About Me

Get to learn more about Julia

I'm a UX Designer and Researcher passionate about creating intuitive, user-centered experiences that bridge technology and human needs. Currently, I'm pursuing my Master's degree in User Experience Research and Design at the University of Michigan School of Information, with a Bachelors in UX Design and minors in Entrepreneurship and Native American Studies.

My design philosophy centers on empathy and inclusivity. Whether I'm designing predictive maintenance systems for Stellantis, reimagining in-flight anxiety management with SkyMood, or amplifying survivor voices for Sahiyo's nonprofit work, I believe in creating solutions that genuinely improve people's lives.

What excites me most about UX design is the intersection of creativity, problem-solving, and social impact. I love diving deep into user research, uncovering insights that lead to meaningful design decisions, and iterating until we get it right.

Beyond design, I'm passionate about entrepreneurship and community building. My study abroad experiences in Copenhagen and Barcelona broadened my perspective on design thinking and business strategy, while my involvement in organizations like Michigan Build and Launch and the Michigan Open UX Fellowship has allowed me to mentor and collaborate with fellow designers.
