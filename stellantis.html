AI-Driven Predictive Maintenance System

Designing the future of automotive maintenance for Stellantis

Role

UX Designer

Timeline

Aug 2024 - Dec 2024

Tools

Figma, User Research

Team

Stellantis Partnership

Problem Statement

Vehicles currently struggle with reactive maintenance approaches that lead to unexpected breakdowns and high repair costs.

Can an AI-driven predictive maintenance system provide proactive recommendations to minimize downtime and reduce repair expenses?

Solution

Partnered with Stellantis to design an AI-driven predictive maintenance UX system that monitors vehicle health in real time, delivering actionable insights to drivers while reducing repair costs and vehicle downtime.

The system includes:

  • Mobile App: Pre-trip diagnostics, driving feedback, and maintenance alerts
  • In-Car Dashboard: Real-time vehicle health monitoring and AI-powered maintenance scheduling
  • Multi-Modal Interface: Seamless integration across phone and vehicle displays

Mobile Experience

Home & Vehicle Controls

The mobile app home screen provides quick access to vehicle controls, alerts, and driving feedback. Users can unlock/lock their vehicle, view maintenance needs, and access their driving history.

Driving Feedback & AI Diagnostics

The app analyzes weekly driving patterns and provides personalized recommendations to improve driving habits. The AI diagnostics feature shows engine health percentage and provides timely maintenance recommendations, such as oil changes, to protect vehicle longevity.

In-Car Dashboard Experience

Driver Display & Center Console

The in-car experience features a comprehensive driver display showing speedometer, mileage, and vehicle mode visualization alongside a center console with calendar integration and maintenance alerts.

AI-Generated Maintenance Alerts

When the system detects maintenance needs, AI-generated pop-ups allow users to schedule appointments directly from their calendar. The system proactively reminds drivers about upcoming maintenance to prevent unexpected issues.

Smart Navigation Features

The navigation system goes beyond basic directions by incorporating:

  • Nearby Services: Shows gas stations and EV charging locations when resting
  • AI Traffic Alerts: Suggests eco-friendly alternative routes during traffic
  • Road Hazard Detection: Alerts drivers to potholes and road conditions with alternative route options
  • Real-Time Updates: Displays arrival time, distance, and turn-by-turn directions

Key Design Decision: Maintenance Hub

The Maintenance Hub serves as the central location for all vehicle health information. It displays:

  • Current maintenance needs with urgency indicators
  • Maintenance history with costs and dates
  • 360° vehicle visualization
  • Repair cost estimates and timing recommendations

This consolidated view helps users understand their vehicle's health status at a glance and make informed decisions about maintenance timing and budgeting.

Design System & Style Guide

Brand Integration

The design system adheres to Citroën's brand guidelines while creating a modern, accessible interface:

  • Logos: Consistent use of Citroën chevron branding in red and white
  • Typography: Trust Gothic Next LT Pro for hierarchy and readability
  • Color Palette: Pure white, Mason gray, Hex red, and black for clear contrast
  • Icons: Comprehensive library covering vehicle functions, navigation, and system indicators

Impact

This project represents a significant step forward in automotive UX design by:

  • Creating a multi-modal interface that seamlessly integrates mobile and in-car experiences
  • Leveraging AI and machine learning to provide proactive maintenance recommendations
  • Enhancing safety and convenience through predictive analytics
  • Reducing vehicle downtime and repair costs through early detection
  • Designing for the 2030 automotive market with scalable, future-proof solutions

Key Achievements

Developed comprehensive UX solutions aligned with Stellantis brand guidelines that enhance vehicle longevity and driver confidence through predictive maintenance.
