RoosRoast Coffee: E-Commerce Redesign

Transforming a local coffee roaster's digital presence

Role

UX Designer

Timeline

Jan 2024 - Mar 2024

Tools

Figma, Usability Testing

Team

2 UX Designers

Executive Summary

RoosRoast is more than a coffee company—it's a cafe and a community. Our goal was to redesign their e-commerce website to guide users through a virtual RoosRoast store, displaying products with detailed information to aid selection while creating an appealing subscriptions page to encourage customer loyalty.

Project Goals

  • Guide users through a virtual RoosRoast store experience
  • Display products with detailed information to aid selection
  • Communicate pricing clearly and transparently
  • Create an appealing subscriptions page to encourage loyalty

Research & Analysis

Client Engagement

We conducted comprehensive research, analysis, and redesign in collaboration with our teammate Claire Callen. Our process included:

  • Store visits to understand the physical experience
  • Interviews with the CEO to align on business goals
  • Competitive analysis of industry leaders

Competitive Analysis

We analyzed both coffee and non-coffee e-commerce sites to identify best practices:

Non-Coffee Benchmarks

  • Nike Search Results: Filters on left side, left-aligned text, bolded price
  • Book of the Month Subscriptions: Large heading, three benefits with images, clear "how it works" section

Coffee Industry Benchmarks

  • Peet's Coffee: Clear buying options with size/quantity selectors and flavor profiles
  • Verve Coffee Roasters: Roast and flavor hints beneath products with left-aligned text and large images

Problem Statement

The shopping cart and checkout process are confusing to users - the cart doesn't show a clear checkout button and no 'save for later' option.

Subscription information is unclear. When it comes to subscriptions, information on subscription options, pricing, and content is not communicated well.

The site struggles to translate RoosRoast's identity. Overall, the current site struggles to convey what makes RoosRoast special through both its e-commerce functionalities and ability to resonate with the company's identity.

Key Design Decisions

1. Homepage Redesign

We completely reimagined the homepage to bridge the in-person experience with the online presence:

  • Ensure a broad selection of different product types are featured
  • Use bespoke homepage imagery and design to set a positive first impression
  • Convey RoosRoast's brand value and community focus

2. Optimizing the Shopping Bag

We redesigned the shopping cart to address major usability issues:

  • Clearly display the subtotal and total with prominent placement
  • Add a 'save for later' option for items customers want to consider
  • Place saved items at the bottom of the cart for easy access
  • Highlight the checkout button with strong visual emphasis

3. Clarifying the Subscription Process

The subscription page was completely reimagined to encourage customer loyalty:

  • Create a "How it Works" page that provides more clarity on the subscriptions process
  • Make the subscription page easily accessible to users throughout the site
  • Reduce information overload with clear, digestible sections
  • Use compelling visuals to communicate subscription benefits

Before & After: Complete Redesign

Homepage Transformation

The homepage evolved from a cluttered, image-heavy layout to a clean, organized interface showcasing products and brand storytelling with clear navigation.

Search & Product Pages

We transformed the search results from a simple grid to an organized view with comprehensive filters:

  • Filters: Flavor, price, roast level, size, brewing method
  • Better Visualization: Larger product images with key information
  • Improved Layout: Clear hierarchy and easy scanning

Product pages were enhanced with:

  • Multiple high-quality images
  • Comprehensive product details and flavor profiles
  • Brewing suggestions
  • Recommended products section

Checkout Process

The checkout flow was simplified with clearer form fields, better organization, and a more intuitive multi-step process.

Design System & Style Guide

We created a comprehensive design system to ensure consistency across all pages and future development:

Visual Identity

  • Color Palette: Warm, inviting colors that reflect RoosRoast's community focus
  • Typography: Clear hierarchy with readable fonts
  • Imagery: Authentic, high-quality photos showcasing products and cafe atmosphere
  • Iconography: Simple, recognizable icons for navigation and features

Responsive Design

All designs were created for both mobile and desktop platforms, ensuring a seamless experience across devices. We developed 5 key frames for each platform:

  • Homepage
  • Search Results
  • Product Page
  • Shopping Cart
  • Checkout

Usability Testing & Validation

We applied style guides and conducted rigorous usability testing through the Bayard Institute to validate design decisions and ensure an optimal user experience.

Key Testing Insights

  • Users responded positively to the cleaner, more organized layout
  • The "save for later" feature was highly valued by testers
  • Subscription information clarity significantly improved comprehension
  • Navigation became more intuitive with the redesigned structure

Testing Results

Usability testing confirmed that the redesigned interface successfully addressed the original problems: users could easily find products, understand pricing, navigate checkout, and comprehend subscription options.

Client Presentation & Delivery

We delivered a comprehensive client presentation detailing our design decisions and recommendations:

Key Deliverables

  • Complete redesigned website (5 key screens)
  • Intuitive subscription page design to drive customer loyalty
  • Solutions to confusing user flows
  • Style guide for consistent future development
  • Responsive designs for mobile and desktop

Client Feedback

The CEO and team at RoosRoast responded enthusiastically to the redesign, particularly appreciating how the new design captured their brand identity while improving functionality.

Impact & Results

The RoosRoast e-commerce redesign successfully achieved all project goals:

  • Improved User Experience: Clear navigation and intuitive flows throughout the site
  • Enhanced Brand Identity: Design resonates with RoosRoast's community-focused values
  • Better Product Discovery: Users can easily find and learn about products
  • Subscription Clarity: Transparent pricing and benefits drive customer loyalty
  • Optimized Checkout: Streamlined process reduces cart abandonment

Key Achievement

The redesign successfully bridges the gap between RoosRoast's beloved in-person cafe experience and their digital presence, creating a cohesive brand experience that encourages both online purchases and community engagement.

Key Learnings

This project taught us valuable lessons about e-commerce UX design:

  • Context Matters: Understanding the physical store experience informed our digital design decisions
  • Simplicity Wins: Reducing complexity in checkout and subscriptions improved usability dramatically
  • Brand Authenticity: Maintaining brand identity while improving functionality creates emotional connection
  • Competitive Analysis Value: Studying both coffee and non-coffee sites provided diverse insights
  • Iterative Testing: Multiple rounds of feedback led to continuous improvement
