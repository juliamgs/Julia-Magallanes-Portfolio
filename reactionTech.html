Reaction Technologies: Athlete Training App

Designing a mobile app to guide athletes through sport-specific drills

Role

UX Designer

Timeline

Jun 2024 - Aug 2024

Tools

Figma

Context

Ann Arbor SPARK Digital Summer Clinic

Project Overview

Reaction Technologies is a sports tech startup focused on improving athletic performance through personalized training. During the Ann Arbor SPARK Digital Summer Clinic, I designed and prototyped a mobile application that guides athletes through sport-specific drills tailored to their needs.

Problem & Opportunity

Athletes need accessible, personalized training guidance that adapts to their sport, skill level, and training goals. Traditional training apps lack the specificity and customization that serious athletes require.

The opportunity lies in a $19 billion total addressable market (TAM) for sports training and performance technology, with potential to reach 20,000 users by 2026.

Design Process

High-Fidelity Prototype Development

I transformed low-fidelity wireframes into polished, detailed designs using Figma, focusing on key screens that create a seamless user experience:

  • Sign Up / Login: Streamlined onboarding to get athletes started quickly
  • Home Screen: Dashboard showing recent activity and recommended drills
  • Use App as Control: Real-time drill guidance with video demonstrations
  • Performance Tracking: Analytics and progress monitoring

Key Features

Sport-Specific Drill Library

Athletes can access a comprehensive library of drills tailored to their specific sport, with video demonstrations and detailed instructions for proper technique.

Interactive Training Sessions

The app guides athletes through training sessions with real-time feedback, timers, and rep counters to ensure they're performing drills correctly and efficiently.

Progress Tracking & Analytics

Users can monitor their improvement over time with detailed analytics showing completion rates, performance metrics, and areas for improvement.

Stakeholder Engagement

Throughout the project, I presented designs to 15+ stakeholders including founders, potential investors, and industry advisors. This iterative feedback process helped refine the app's functionality and ensure it addressed real market needs.

Key outcomes from stakeholder presentations:

  • Validated product-market fit within the $19B TAM
  • Refined user flows based on athlete feedback
  • Identified critical features for MVP launch
  • Established roadmap toward 20K users by 2026

Website Accessibility Improvements

Beyond the mobile app, I advised the founders on improvements to their website, with particular focus on addressing essential accessibility issues:

  • Color contrast ratios for WCAG compliance
  • Keyboard navigation and focus indicators
  • Screen reader compatibility
  • Alt text for images and media content
  • Responsive design improvements for mobile devices

Impact on Accessibility

These improvements ensured that the website would be usable by athletes with diverse abilities, expanding the potential user base and demonstrating commitment to inclusive design.

UI Refinement & Interactive Elements

I meticulously crafted and polished all UI elements to ensure they aligned with the app's design language and provided a sleek, professional look. The interface incorporates:

  • Clickable elements with clear affordances
  • Smooth transitions between screens
  • Realistic navigation flows
  • Consistent visual hierarchy
  • Athletic-focused color palette and typography

Results & Impact

The Reaction Technologies mobile app design successfully:

  • Created a comprehensive prototype ready for user testing and development
  • Positioned the startup to target the $19B sports training market
  • Established user flows optimized for athlete engagement and retention
  • Provided accessibility recommendations to ensure inclusive product development
  • Set foundation for reaching 20,000 users by 2026

Key Takeaway

This project demonstrated how thoughtful UX design can transform a sports tech concept into a market-ready product that addresses real athlete needs while maintaining scalability for growth.
